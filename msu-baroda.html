Overview

Established in 1881 as Kalabhavan Technical Institute and later becoming the Faculty of Technology and Engineering (FTE) of The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda in 1949, it is one of the premier engineering institutions in Gujarat.

Located in the heart of Vadodara, MSU Baroda offers high-quality engineering education supported by a legacy of experienced faculty, a vibrant campus life, and an extensive alumni network.

B.E. Admission Process & Quota

Admission to the B.E. programs at MSU Baroda is managed entirely through the centralized Admission Committee for Professional Courses (ACPC) process:

  • ACPC State Quota: 100% of the Grant-in-Aid (GIA) and Higher Payment seats are filled through ACPC counselling based on the merit of GUJCET and Class 12th Board marks.
  • TFWS Quota: Additional 5% supernumerary seats are available under the Tuition Fee Waiver Scheme (TFWS) for meritorious students.
  • Seat Types: MSU offers both Grant-in-Aid (GIA) seats which are highly subsidized, and Higher Payment seats (for specific branches like Computer Science & Engineering) which have different fee structures.

Fees Structure & Placements

B.E. Fee Structure

As a Grant-in-Aid university, MSU Baroda offers education at highly subsidized rates for most programs. The approximate tuition fee for the regular B.E. programs is:

  • Boys: ₹ 1,500 per annum
  • Girls: Tuition Fee Waiver (Only nominal term fees apply)

Note: Fees for Higher Payment seats (like B.E. Computer Science & Engineering - Higher Payment) are different and can be approximately ₹ 1,00,000 to ₹ 1,20,000 per annum.

Placement Highlights

The placement cell at the Faculty of Technology and Engineering, MSU Baroda is highly active and attracts reputed core engineering companies and IT multinationals.

  • Highest Package: ₹ 15.0 - 20.0 LPA
  • Average Package: ₹ 5.0 - 6.0 LPA
  • Top Recruiters: Reliance Industries, L&T, GSFC, GNFC, MG Motors, TCS, Siemens, Torrent Power, Adani Group, etc.
