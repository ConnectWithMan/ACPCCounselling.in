Admission Phases

The ACPC B.Arch admission process is systematically divided into several phases to ensure a fair and transparent seat allocation for all candidates. Being aware of the schedule helps candidates complete crucial steps on time without missing any deadlines.

The primary phases of the ACPC counselling process include:

  • Online Registration & Document Upload: Candidates register on the portal and submit the necessary documents for verification.
  • Provisional Merit List Declaration: ACPC releases a state-level merit list based on NATA scores and Class 12 board marks.
  • Mock Round Choice Filling: A practice round to give students an idea of potential seat allotments based on their choices.
  • Actual Seat Allotment (Rounds 1 & 2): Final seat allocation where students must confirm their admission by paying the token fee.

Key Instructions for Candidates

Document Readiness

Ensure that all required documents such as Marksheets, Caste Certificates (if applicable), Income Certificates, and EWS Certificates are valid and ready before the registration phase begins. Invalid documents can lead to the cancellation of registration.

Adherence to Deadlines

The ACPC software strictly locks functionalities after the deadline passes. For instance, if you do not lock your choices before the end date of choice filling, the system will auto-lock your last saved choices. Therefore, it is highly recommended not to wait for the last minute.
