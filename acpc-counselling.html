ACPC Counselling 2026

Complete Admission & Seat Allotment Process

For B.E./B.Tech, Pharmacy, Architecture & MBA/MCA in Gujarat

What is ACPC Counselling?

The Admission Committee for Professional Courses (ACPC), Gujarat, conducts the centralized online counselling process for admission into various professional degree courses. Through ACPC Counselling 2026, eligible candidates can secure seats in Government, Grant-in-Aid, and Self-Financed institutions across Gujarat for programs like B.E./B.Tech, B.Pharm, D.Pharm, B.Arch, MBA, and MCA. Admissions are strictly based on merit, evaluated using state and national level entrance exams such as GUJCET, JEE Main, NATA, NEET, and CMAT.

Complete ACPC Counselling Process 2026

The online admission and counselling process for ACPC 2026 involves several key stages. Candidates must complete each step within the stipulated deadlines:

  • 1. Online Registration: Candidates must create an account, fill in personal and academic details, and pay the registration fee online.
  • 2. Document Verification: Upload required documents (marksheets, category certificates). Verification is conducted online (e-verification).
  • 3. Merit List Declaration: ACPC releases a provisional merit list, followed by a final merit list based on qualifying exam and entrance test scores.
  • 4. Choice Filling (Mock & Actual): Candidates select and arrange their preferred colleges and courses. A mock round helps candidates understand the trends before the actual locking of choices.
  • 5. Seat Allotment: Seats are allocated based on merit rank, category reservations, and locked choices.
  • 6. Admission Confirmation: Candidates satisfied with their allotment must pay the token tuition fee online and download their admission letter.
  • 7. Reporting to College: Finally, candidates must report to the allotted institute with original documents to commence classes.

General Eligibility Criteria

To participate in ACPC Counselling, candidates must fulfill specific academic and domicile requirements:

  • For B.E. / B.Tech: Passed 12th Science with Physics and Mathematics. Valid GUJCET or JEE Main score required.
  • For B.Pharm / D.Pharm: Passed 12th Science (PCB/PCM). Valid GUJCET or NEET score required.
  • For B.Arch: Passed 12th with PCM or 10+3 Diploma with Mathematics. Valid NATA score is mandatory.
  • For MBA / MCA: Bachelor's degree with a minimum of 50% marks (45% for reserved categories). Valid CMAT score required.
  • Domicile: Candidates who have passed their qualifying examination from schools located in Gujarat are generally eligible. AICTE approved state quotas apply.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who conducts the ACPC Counselling?

The counselling is conducted by the Admission Committee for Professional Courses (ACPC), Government of Gujarat.

What is the registration fee for ACPC Counselling 2026?

The registration fee is generally Rs. 350/-, which must be paid online and is non-refundable.

Do I need to visit the help center physically for document verification?

No, document verification is primarily done online (e-verification). You only need to physically report to the allotted institute with original documents after the final seat allotment.

What is a mock round in ACPC Counselling?

A mock round is a trial choice-filling phase. It helps candidates understand the seat allotment trends and gives them an idea of which college they might get based on their merit rank before the actual seat allotment.
